◆ fix_collision()

void() fix_collision ( )

Fixes the collision between the panda and the collided item.

This function fixes the collision between the panda and the collided item by adjusting the panda's position. If the collision is on the right side of the item, the panda moves to the left; if the collision is on the left side, the panda moves to the right.
